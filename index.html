About me


Hi! Thank you for visiting my website. My name is Raul Ignatius. I was born in Bangalore in 2005. I have loved cooking every since i can remember. I started helping my mother in the kitchen when i was 5 years old and i started cooking on my own since i was 12. Since then i have been creating and testing out my own recipes in Italian, American, French, Indian and Anglo-Indian Cuisnes. Some failed while some were very succesful. With this i got a wealth of experience in cooking and creating recies and then decided to do cooking for my project in school. That was the part of the reason i wanted to create a website to showcase my own recipes. The other reason is to share my recipes with world and promote the Anglo-Indian cuisine. This is why in this Website you will find no ads or no paid endorsements. It's just me sharing my recipes with the world.
